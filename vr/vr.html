Gathering of Birds

Microsoft x Tel Aviv Museum of Art
x Reichman University

AR team lead

An interactive experience for visitors of Shira Zelwer's 'Gathering of Birds' exhibition. Using augmented reality (AR) technology, the exhibition's birds come to life, allowing visitors to interact with them and learn more about different species. The AR experience is shareable on social media, which helps to increase the museum's exposure to a younger audience.

The Trolley Dilemma

Virtual Reality Development Course

Developer - using Unreal Engine via Blueprints

In the midterm project for the course, I designed and programmed a VR psychology experiment, in collabiration with PSY school, where the user faces the trolley dilemma. A train is coming your way, on the train tracks lay 5 people. you can change the train direction so it will hit only 1 person. Will you do it?
